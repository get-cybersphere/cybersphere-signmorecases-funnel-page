**Paris Law Group (Jason P.)** is confirmed in our internal client memory.

2. Paris is real, TopDog and Rafi are aspirational placeholders intended to be replaced before scaling traffic. This is FTC §255 endorsement-guides exposure as long as they are public.

**Required action before any meaningful traffic hits this page:**

- Confirm written consent on file from James Helm (TopDog Law) and Mike Rafi (Rafi Law) for the verbatim quotes published on the page.
- If consent does not exist, either pull the quotes immediately or replace with the FTC disclosure: "Design-matched illustration of representative client outcomes. Individual results vary."
- Update internal client memory with TopDog Law and Rafi Law if these are real wins.

This is the kind of issue that does not show up in conversion data but blows up in your face on a single LinkedIn post from a competitor.

## 5. Live Offer Voice vs. Brand Book Voice

The live page is in **direct-response cold-traffic voice**. The brand book is in **engineered-premium warm-traffic voice**. Both are valid, but they need to be reconciled into a coherent funnel structure.

| Element | Live page | Brand book |
|---|---|---|
| H1 framing | "Implement Our AI Intake Machine To Get 10+ Signed Cases" | "Sign more cases. Pay only when they do." |
| CTA | "Apply For Your Growth Strategy Call To See If You Qualify" | "Book the strategy call" |
| Voice | Loud, urgent, qualifying-gate | Confident, technical, peer-level |
| Aesthetic | Black + gold + yellow accents | Cream + ink + signal red |
| Trust signal | "100+ Reviews on Trustpilot" | "$4.2M signed retainers · Paris Law Group · 90 days" |

**The reconciliation:**

The live page is doing the job of a cold-traffic Meta-ad landing page (qualifying-gate, urgent, high-stakes-feeling). That's defensible. But it does not match the warm-traffic / partner-pitch positioning we built the brand book around.

**Recommended split:**

- **Cold-traffic landing page** (current live page, refined): keeps the direct-response voice but applies the new color/type system. Use "Apply" framing because cold buyers respond to qualifying gates.
- **Warm-traffic / referral landing page** (yet to build): uses the full brand book voice, "Book the strategy call · 25 min · No pitch," premium editorial layout.
- **Sales pages, decks, partner pitches:** full brand book voice, no question.

The brand book becomes the system. The live page becomes one variant within that system.

## 7. Brand DNA (paste-ready)

### Voice principles
1. Direct. Lead with the claim. Proof follows.
2. Technical without being jargony.
3. Engineer-not-marketer.
4. Short sentences. Single ideas per line.
5. Specific numbers. "Sub-10-second response," not "fast."
6. Confident, not boastful.
7. Lawyer-respectful (PI partners are sophisticated buyers).

### Banned words/phrases
leverage · synergize · world-class · best-in-class · cutting-edge · innovative · transform · unlock · harness · empower · revolutionary · "we are passionate about" · "in today's competitive landscape" · em dashes in body copy · exclamation points in body copy · hedging words ("could," "might," "may help")

### Required brand verbs/nouns
**Sign** (cases sign, signed retainers) · **Engineered** · **Retainer** (the unit of value, never "lead") · **Pay only when** · **Sub-10-second** · **In-house / built in-house**

### Five messaging pillars
1. AI-native, not human-pretending-AI
2. Pay only when cases sign
3. Speed is the product
4. Engineered, not outsourced
5. Every PI vertical (auto, premises, mass tort, med mal, workers' comp)

## 13. Compliance Posture

PI marketing has bar-association exposure. These rules are non-negotiable:

- **Never guarantee outcomes.** "We will sign 20 cases for you" is exposed. "We deliver pay-per-signed-case infrastructure that has signed X cases for Y firms" is fine.
- **Never call leads "clients" until retained.** Use "leads," "matters," "retainers."
- **State-specific advertising rules** apply per client (Florida, Texas, New York have stricter regimes).
- **FTC §255 endorsement-guides:** every testimonial must be either real and consented or visibly labeled as a representative illustration.

**Standard footer disclaimer (use verbatim):**
> Sign More Cases provides marketing and intake infrastructure for personal injury law firms. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice. Performance figures cited reflect the experience of representative client firms. Individual results vary based on geography, case type, ad spend, and intake operations.
